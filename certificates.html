Graphic

GTQ Photoshop

This is an interactive example illustrating SDG Indicator 2.1.1 as a proportion of millions of people in both 2010 and 2020. The map was created by plotting the cleaned data with Microsoft Excel and utilizing ArcGIS Online to generate an instant app with interactive features. By examining the map, you can identify areas that have undergone significant changes between the two time periods. These changes may be reflected in variations in the prevalence of undernourishment across different regions. Some areas may have experienced improvements, indicating a reduction in the proportion of undernourished individuals, while others may have witnessed deterioration, suggesting an increase in undernourishment levels.
In which area do you notice the most significant changes? Which area showed improvement or deterioration? What could be the reasons behind these changes?
