In the Masters of Geospatial Technology in the Urban Studies program I developed and taught the courses Web GIS, Mobile GIS and Environmental Planning Applications. I have also taught Introduction to Geospatial Technologies in 2014. At the undergraduate level, I taught Introduction to Urban Research Methods, and developed and implemented the course Urban Remote Sensing.
