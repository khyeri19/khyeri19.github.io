Mobile Maps

Mobile maps fit in your back pocket!

Maps that are used on mobile devices require special consideration and planning in terms of design and technical considerations. The mobile user has a small screen and is often not giving the screen their full attention because they are walking or there are other things going on around them.

Read more about mobile maps and responsive design in the chapter of the Geographic Information Science Body of Knowledge I co-wrote with Rob Roth.
I also wrote the chapter titled mobile devices in the Geographic Information Science Body of Knowledge which focuses on considerations about how to develop mobile map application that features maps.
